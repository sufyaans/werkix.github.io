About Us

Werkix was founded in response to a lack of real opportunities for tech talent in Malawi.
In a market dominated by talent from the Far East and with Malawi’s small economy, we observed that hundreds of tech graduates were employed in roles unrelated to their degrees. Computer scientists found themselves in sales jobs, for example, while others tried their hand as entrepreneurs.
Exacerbating this problem was a lack of awareness of the remote job market. Graduates were not aware of its potential and were not trained to take advantage of it in any case.
We also discovered that the best techies who did have jobs were often on the lookout for a side hustle to boost their income. They desired the freedom of a remote position to work after hours, but the framework that would support this arrangement did not exist.
Sensing an opportunity to address a gap in the market, we decided to make local African tech talent available to the rest of the world.
This would have obvious benefits for graduates and those who wanted to earn extra income, but it would also benefit companies in search of a hands-off remote talent solution.
Today, Werkix helps businesses avoid the often tedious task of managing remote talent. We employ a rigorous screening process to shortlist the best software developers, cyber security experts, network engineers, and web developers.
New hires are incubated in our central office in Blantyre, Malawi, Africa, but we also supervise and onboard hires in Belgrade, London, New York, and Johannesburg.

Our Process

First, we jump on a call with you to better understand the job and your company. We understand that each position and company is unique and requires a tailored approach. Werkix will then find the best recruits who are committed to your cause, culture, and company values. In other words, we look for people who will be excited to work for you. We offer full time hires first and foremost, but there is also an option for those that are already employed. These are often individuals looking to work on a side hustle after hours and, as a result, do not tend to appear on job boards.

We will then conduct our rigorous screening process and assemble a list of candidates to ensure each is ready to work remotely.

We first conduct an internal interview and then schedule an interview with the client. This ensures you are comfortable that the candidate is a good fit before moving forward. Our team also analyzes a candidate’s background before we commit to hiring. This includes checks on their: Soft skills, Motivation and career plans, Level of English language competence, and Feedback from past colleagues.

There is a hand holding period where we introduce and welcome new hires to the company and ensure they know how to use any proprietary tools. Individuals are trained on office etiquette and Werkix will provide upskilling on certain technical skills if required. To ensure tasks are completed on time and quality standards are upheld, hires work under the supervision of a team leader and specialized monitoring software.
